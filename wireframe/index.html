Mission

Committed to helping those who are most in need to succeed. Our purpose is to improve the lives of people in our community through assessment, attitude and job skills training, and high potential career placement.

Philosophy

JobTrain is founded on a belief in the potential of every human being. We believe that through training, encouragement and inspiration, our trainees will be empowered to become self-supporting and thriving members of society. We believe in the dignity and potential of every individual. We believe that with opportunity and motivation, people will help themselves. We believe that people need and want a hand up, not a hand out.
